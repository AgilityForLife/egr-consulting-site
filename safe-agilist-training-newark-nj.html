Frequently Asked Questions

What is SAFe Agilist certification and who should get it?

SAFe Agilist (SA) is the foundational credential for leaders, executives, managers, and change agents who drive Lean-Agile transformations at enterprise scale. It's earned by completing the Leading SAFe course and passing the SA exam. Project managers, Scrum Masters, product managers, directors, and PMO leaders across New Jersey and the tri-state area pursue it to lead SAFe adoption with credibility.

How long does it take to get SAFe Agilist certified in New Jersey?

Most students earn the Certified SAFe Agilist credential in 2 to 3 weeks. The course itself is a 2-day intensive. You then have 30 days of included exam access. We help Newark, Bloomfield, and Jersey City students prepare and pass on the first attempt.

What is the difference between SAFe Agilist and Certified Scrum Master?

Certified Scrum Master focuses on a single Scrum team. SAFe Agilist focuses on scaling Agile across programs, portfolios, and the entire enterprise — multiple teams, budgeting, PI Planning, Agile Release Trains, and Lean governance. If you operate or plan to operate above the team level, SAFe Agilist is the more strategic credential.
