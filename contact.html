Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond?

All emails and form submissions are answered within one business day. For fastest response, use the Book a Call page to schedule directly.

Do you work with individuals or only organizations?

Both! Individual certification prep (PMP, SAFe) is open to anyone, and consulting/corporate training is available for teams of any size — from 2-person startups to Fortune 500s.

Are training programs available in-person?

Most programs are delivered live virtually (Zoom/Teams). On-site delivery at your location is available for corporate engagements in the NJ/NY metro area and beyond by arrangement.

What is the consulting engagement model?

Engagements are scoped and priced based on program complexity, duration, and team size. We offer embedded, advisory, and fractional PM and agile coaching models. Get a proposal via a free strategy call.
