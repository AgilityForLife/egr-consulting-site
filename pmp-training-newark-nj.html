Frequently Asked Questions About PMP Training

What are the prerequisites for PMP certification? +

The PMI (Project Management Institute) requires applicants to meet education and experience requirements. Generally, you need either a secondary degree (high school diploma or equivalent) with 5 years of project management experience, or a bachelor's degree with 3 years of experience. Additionally, you must complete 35 hours of project management training. If you're unsure whether you qualify, we'll review your background during your free consultation and help you understand your eligibility for the PMP exam.

How long does PMP training typically take? +

Most professionals complete PMP certification training within 6 to 12 weeks, depending on their current knowledge level, study pace, and the format they choose.
